Mission Statement

The core of good problem-solving lies in looking at the bigger picture. Before I start on how, I start with asking why. Answer to the why gives the perspective of the impact it has & it gives a baseline on what is the goal to achieve by solving the problem. I believe when the big picture is always in mind, we tend to stay focused on the required solution & not over-engineer things. Often solution architects are carried away by implementing elegant design patterns & the latest trends, which may have been solved by taking a simple approach. My work philosophy is to find the simple approach to solution architecture that is scalable, maintainable & performant. I also believe collaboration is key to breaking down a complex problem to smaller pieces, that leads to the simpler architecture to solve for it.
